Johnny Depp - Raoul Duke

Johnny Depp is an American actor, producer and musician. He was born in Owensboro, Kentucky in 1963. He is one of the most talented and highest paid actors in the world. Depp started his career back in 1984 starring in a horror movie A Nightmare on Elm Street. Since then he played in many big productions and iconic movies such as: Edward Scissorhands, Public Enemies, Charlie and The Chocolate Family, Donnie Brasco, Sweeney Todd, Alice in Wonderland and the successful Disney film series Pirates of the Caribbean. Morever he did voice to many animated films like Rango or Corpse Bride. Throughout his career he has been nominated to many important awards and managed to win a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor - Motion Picture Musical or Comedy for Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street in 2008.

In Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas Johnny Depp plays a cynical journalist Raoul Duke who is not scared of any psychoactive substances and lives his daily life while being under influence of whatever drugs happens to be available.

Benicio Del Toro - Dr. Gonzo

Benicio Del Toro who is Puerto Rican actor and producer is mostly know for his unique charismatic character. He was born on 19th of February in 1967. He started his career by playing small television roles during the late 1980s. Benicio gained mainstream audience after his performance in Steven Soderbergh's Traffic in year 2000 where he played a conflicted mexican policeman. During his incredible career he received many important awards and nominations. In last 30 years his acting performance made him win a total of 21 awards including an Academy Award, Golden Globe Award, British Academy Film Award and two Screen Actors Guild Awards. The movies that gained him the most of his recognition are Traffic, 21 Grams, Fearless, Guardians of the Galaxy and Sicario.

In Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas Benicio performed as drug-addicted attorney of sportswriter Raoul Duke. Whatsmore he even gained almost 20 kilograms while preparing for his role.
